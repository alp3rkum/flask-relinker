Help

Flask Relinker is a simple yet powerful URL shortener application that is powered with Flask and SQLite at its heart. This minimalistic, casual user-oriented application offers everything you could need to shorten and manage the URLs of your favorite websites.

For a normal user, this is just anothe small application making user of TinyURL API to shorten links. But if you...

Become A Member

Then you get some good perks for doing so! All you have to do is to enter a username, your e-mail adress and a good password for yourself, and in return you can:

  • Shorten your URLs with a custom algorithm (which uses SHA-384 hash algorithm!).
  • Save and copy up to 10 of your favorite URLs in their shortened form.
  • Access the Top 10 most converted links page, just in case you need a popular website's URL in shortened form. Who knows, maybe your favorite website will be there one day!

No need to sync, no paid features, no interrupting advertisements, and no buzzword marketing tricks to manipulate you into being a member, paid or not. Just a simple, back-to-basics, self-developed URL shortener that does its job good.
